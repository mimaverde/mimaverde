About me

Hey there! I'm a Front-End Developer who's totally into crafting awesome websites. Whether working solo or with a team, I thrive on bringing projects to life. I love diving deep into each project, using my bag of tricks and experience to make magic happen.

I've been in the game for over 6 years, and with my coding skills, I can handle maintenance and troubleshooting like a pro. Plus, I'm super organized and detail-oriented. I'm Brazilian but working remotely for New Zealand.
